## 2. WHO THE VOICE IS

**The Caribbean big sister side:** Warm but direct. Doesn't perform warmth, IS warm. Tells you the truth because she loves you, not despite it. Will check you when you're spiralling. Will sit with you when you're done. Doesn't sugarcoat. Doesn't pile on.

**The regulated baddie side:** Grounded. Doesn't move from a place of urgency or fear. Knows her worth without needing to prove it. Gets to the point. Makes decisions. Holds her line.

**Together:** A woman who has been through it, processed it, and now speaks with both depth and ease. She's not above you. She's not below you. She's beside you, naming what she sees.

## 3. CORE TONE RULES

### Lead with action, then explain
Get to the point fast. Then explain if needed. Never the reverse.

❌ "There are several factors to consider when thinking about your patterns. One thing you might want to look at is..."
✅ "Your work and your identity are tied together. That's the pattern. Here's how it shows up."

### Real words, not corporate or therapy speak

❌ "It appears that you may be experiencing..."
✅ "Here's what's real."

❌ "You might consider..."
✅ "Here's the move."

❌ "Studies suggest..."
✅ "This works because..."

❌ "Lean into your authentic self"
✅ "Stop performing for people who never asked you to"

### Short paragraphs, lots of line breaks
AuDHD-friendly. Scannable. Never wall-of-text.

### Specific, not abstract
Name actual behaviours, not feelings.

❌ "You feel overwhelmed"
✅ "You're saying yes to things you wanted to decline three days ago"

### Witty and blunt, never harsh
Cut through without cruelty. Light cussing when it fits the moment, never to shock.

---

## 4. WHAT THE VOICE NEVER DOES

**Never performs.** No begging, no over-explaining, no qualifying every sentence into mush.

**Never spiritual bypasses.** Doesn't reach for "everything happens for a reason" when someone's hurting. Sits with it instead.

**Never sells with hype.** No "transform your life," no "manifest your dreams," no urgency-fear-scarcity tactics.

**Never rescues.** Offers tools, not interventions. The client is the authority on their lived experience.

**Never preaches.** Grounded wisdom over guru tone. Says "here's what I've seen" not "here's the truth."

**Never flexes.** Smart but doesn't show off. Doesn't quote research to win arguments.

**Never patronises.** Especially the woman reading. Treats her like she's intelligent, even when she's spiralling.

## 6. WHEN TO USE LIGHT CUSSING

Cussing earns its place. Don't sprinkle it for edge.

**Use when:**
- Cutting through someone's denial
- Naming something serious in a way that lands
- Real-talk moments between sister and sister
- "That's bullshit and you know it" moments

**Don't use when:**
- Selling a product
- Writing legal copy
- Talking about clients' patterns (their pattern isn't "fucked up", it's a pattern)
- Compliance-sensitive content

Examples that work:
- "Stop pretending you don't know what you know."
- "That ain't it, sis."
- "The work doesn't owe you ease. It owes you truth."

## 8. RHYTHM AND STRUCTURE

### Sentence length
Mix short and medium. Avoid long compound sentences. If a sentence runs over 25 words, break it.

### Paragraph length
2-4 sentences. Single-sentence paragraphs for emphasis are fine and encouraged.

### Repetition for emphasis
Repeat key phrases when something matters. Don't be afraid of:
- "She's not above you. She's not below you. She's beside you."
- "The work doesn't lie. The work doesn't lie."

### Pull-quote moments
The voice has rhythms that earn highlighting. Build them in.

---

## 9. PLATFORM-SPECIFIC ADJUSTMENTS

### TikTok / video scripts
Conversational. Drop articles when natural. Faster pace. More cussing okay.

### Email
Direct. Subject line is a sentence to her, not a marketing hook. Close warm.

### Reports
Slower. More care. Compliance language always present. Identity framing not behavioural prescription.

### Sales pages
Confidence over hype. Name what it is, name what it isn't. Let it sell itself.

### Admin / internal copy
Tighter, more functional. Voice still present but operational.

---

## 10. COMPLIANCE-VOICE INTEGRATION

The voice never overrides compliance. Three rules always apply, regardless of how warm or blunt the moment is:

- **Non-predictive:** "You might notice this pattern" never "you will experience"
- **Non-diagnostic:** "This is what the pattern looks like" never "you have this condition"
- **Non-prescriptive:** "Here's the move if it fits" never "you must do this"

The voice can be direct without being predictive, diagnostic, or prescriptive. The work is in saying real things while staying within those lines.
