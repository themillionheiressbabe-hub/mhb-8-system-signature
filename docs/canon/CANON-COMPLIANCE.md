# CANON-COMPLIANCE
## The MillionHeiress BABE™ | Locked Compliance Framework
## Version: 1.0 | April 2026

---

## 1. THE FIVE NON-NEGOTIABLES

These apply to every report, every page, every email, every TikTok, every word that goes out under MHB.

| Rule | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Non-predictive** | Pattern recognition, not fortune telling. We read what's there, we don't say what will happen. |
| **Non-diagnostic** | No clinical labels, no pathologising. We name patterns, not conditions. |
| **Non-therapeutic** | Not therapy, not counselling, not clinical intervention. Entertainment and personal development. |
| **Non-prescriptive** | Offer options, never orders. "Here's the move if it fits" not "you must do this." |
| **Non-hierarchical** | Client is authority on their lived experience. The receipts inform. They never override. |

---

## 2. POSITIONING (LEGAL)

MHB is positioned as **entertainment and personal development**. Not:

- Therapy
- Counselling  
- Mental health treatment
- Medical advice
- Financial advice
- Legal advice
- Religious or spiritual counselling

Every product and report carries this positioning. Disclaimers live in legal pages and within reports.

---

## 3. THE CLIENT AUTHORITY RULE

Every report includes some version of:

> *"If your lived experience disagrees with anything in this document, trust your lived experience. The receipts inform. They never override what you know about yourself."*

This isn't optional. This isn't decoration. This is the legal and ethical line that lets the work be powerful without overstepping.

---

## 4. LANGUAGE PATTERNS

### Predictive language (BANNED)

| Don't say | Say instead |
|---|---|
| You will... | You might notice... |
| This means you're going to... | This pattern shows up as... |
| Next month brings... | This period tends to surface... |
| You're going to meet... | This window often holds... |
| Expect to... | You may experience... |

### Diagnostic language (BANNED)

| Don't say | Say instead |
|---|---|
| You have anxiety | This pattern shows up as activation in the body |
| You're traumatised | The data shows you've been holding a lot |
| You have ADHD | Your wiring runs fast across multiple channels |
| You're depressed | The energy reads as withdrawn right now |
| You're an empath | You absorb other people's signals quickly |

We name patterns. We don't name conditions. Even if the pattern matches a clinical condition, we describe it as a pattern.

### Prescriptive language (BANNED)

| Don't say | Say instead |
|---|---|
| You must... | Here's the move if it fits... |
| You should... | One option is... |
| You need to... | What works for this pattern is... |
| You have to... | Try this on... |
| Stop doing... | Notice when you... |

We offer options, not orders. Even when something is clearly not serving them, we point at the pattern, not the person.

## 5. WHAT WE DON'T TALK ABOUT

### Health and medical
- No medical conditions
- No medication advice
- No nutrition prescriptions
- No fitness prescriptions
- No mental health diagnoses
- No fertility predictions
- No pregnancy predictions

If the pattern surfaces something health-related, we name it as a pattern and direct them to a qualified professional.

### Financial
- No investment advice
- No "you'll come into money" predictions
- No business success predictions
- No "best time to launch" predictions

The Wiring Map (Business) reads patterns in how someone works. It doesn't predict outcomes.

### Legal
- No legal advice
- No "you should sue" or "this is your case"

### Romantic predictions
- No "you'll meet someone in October"
- No "this person is your soulmate"
- No "leave them" or "stay"

The Relationship Signature reads patterns in how two people meet. It doesn't predict whether the relationship will work.

### Death and serious life events
- No predictions about death, illness, accidents
- No predictions about anyone other than the client themselves

## 6. THE FEAR-URGENCY-MANIPULATION BAN

We never sell with:

- Fake scarcity ("only 3 spots left")
- Fear of missing out
- "If you don't do this you'll regret it" energy
- Countdown timers on evergreen products
- "Last chance" language unless it's actually the last chance
- "This price is going up" unless it actually is

The work is good. We don't need pressure tactics. The brand outlives any single launch.

---

## 7. THE SHADOW WORK COMPLIANCE LINE

We do shadow work. We name hard things. We don't:

- Pile on someone in pain
- Use shadow patterns to shame
- Imply they're "broken" or "wrong"
- Frame shadow as moral failure

Shadow is data. It's a pattern showing up in a stressed state. It's not who they are. It's what's running when they're not centred.

Voice rule: shadow gets named with care, not relish.

---

## 8. PROTECTED TOPICS (HANDLE WITH SPECIFIC CARE)

### Children, parenting
We can read patterns in how someone parents. We don't predict their child's life.

### Pregnancy, fertility, miscarriage
We don't predict any of these. We don't read patterns about whether someone will conceive.

### Death, loss, grief
We can hold space. We don't predict timing. We don't claim contact with anyone passed.

### Mental health crises
If someone reaches out in crisis, the response is care + redirect to professional help. We don't try to read their pattern in that moment.

### Suicide, self-harm
If anyone mentions either, full stop. Direct them to crisis resources. Do not engage with the topic as a pattern.

### Religion, spiritual trauma
We hold patterns. We don't tell anyone what to believe. We don't argue with their faith.

## 9. AGE-APPROPRIATENESS

MHB is for adults. 18+.

If a minor (or someone reading on behalf of a minor) approaches us:
- We don't read children's data
- We don't make recommendations about children
- We refer them to a qualified family practitioner

## 13. THE LIVE DEBRIEF LINE

If a client books a Live Debrief:

- It is a conversation, not a session
- Yemi is not a therapist, counsellor, or coach
- The conversation explores the report content
- If anything clinical surfaces, we redirect to professional help
- The client is in charge of what they share

---

## 14. THE TWO TESTS

Before anything goes out, two checks:

### Test 1: The Lawyer Test
Could a lawyer point at this and call it medical/financial/legal advice or a prediction? If yes, rewrite.

### Test 2: The Friend Test
If this landed in front of someone going through the worst week of her life, would it land safely? If it could shame her, scare her, or bypass her pain, rewrite.

Both tests have to pass. Always.
