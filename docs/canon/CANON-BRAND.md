## 3. VISUAL IDENTITY: FEMININE CYBERPUNK

The look is **feminine cyberpunk**. Two energies layered, not separated.

**Cyberpunk side:** Deep navy backgrounds, glowing accents, neon-on-dark moments, futuristic edges, tech-forward.

**Feminine side:** Cormorant Garamond serif (editorial, elegant), magenta warmth, gold luxury, soft glow not harsh light, hairline borders.

The two energies live together. Magenta and gold on deep navy reads as cyberpunk silhouette with feminine richness inside it.

### Visual moves that make it land

- Deep navy backgrounds, never pure black
- Magenta glow text-shadow on key headline words
- Gold accent lines, eyebrow labels, hairline borders
- Emerald for living interactive states (the digital pulse)
- Cormorant Garamond serifs glowing in headlines (editorial femininity meets neon)
- Subtle grain or noise texture overlays (filmic, never sterile)
- Pill-shaped buttons with soft magenta glow on hover
- Hairline gold borders at 15% opacity on card edges

### What it is NOT

- Pure black backgrounds (use deep navy)
- Hard chrome edges or harsh tech aesthetics
- Pastel anything
- Cottagecore, boho, soft girl
- Y2K maximalism
- Corporate tech (no Stripe-blue, no SaaS gradient)
- AI purple gradients
- Neon for the sake of neon (every glow earns its place)
